Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 February 2026

Paak is a free packing list app built by Petar Ceklic. Your privacy is important, and Paak is designed to respect it fully.

Data collection

Paak does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All trip information, packing lists, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Nothing leaves your phone.

Network requests

Paak makes network requests to retrieve climate data for your chosen destinations. These requests contain only the destination name and do not include any personal information, device identifiers, or tracking data.

Analytics and tracking

Paak does not use any analytics services, crash reporting tools, or advertising frameworks. There are no trackers of any kind.

Third-party services

Paak does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

Data sharing

Since no personal data is collected, no data is shared with anyone.

Children's privacy

Paak does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions, reach out at petarceklic.com.
